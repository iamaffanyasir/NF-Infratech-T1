Who we are

We are a multi-disciplinary organization specializing in delivering exceptional software and mobility solutions to both governments and enterprises.

As technology continues to develop rapidly, we are dedicated to supporting our clients in achieving their full potential by providing tailored software and hardware-integrated solutions. Our partnerships with top-performing companies have enabled us to excel in various domains, including enterprise and e-governance projects. With a team of highly skilled professionals, we are committed to delivering exceptional national-level implementations that drive success.

NF Infratech is a game-changer in delivering top-notch ICT solutions and driving m-governance and e-governance initiatives across India. With over a decade of experience, we have become the go-to partners for global tech giants. We're also recognized GeM sellers who strive to shape a digitally inclusive future. Our mission is to empower digital India through innovative ICT solutions, and we've got a proven track record of delivering pioneering solutions for the government and enterprise empowerment. What sets us apart is our team of experts who are dedicated to providing customized

services that meet the unique needs of our clients. We understand the challenges and opportunities in the Indian technology landscape and are committed to delivering tailored solutions. Our secret to success is a perfect blend of innovation, expertise, and commitment. We're always exploring new technologies and methodologies to ensure that our services remain at the forefront of the industry. We take pride in our contribution to the digital transformation of India and are committed to continuing our efforts to shape a digitally inclusive future for the country.

Our Management Team

Mr. Mohammed Hussain

Mr. Mohammed Hussain is a technocrat with extensive experience of heading businesses for Fortune 500 brands like Panasonic & Semsung. His strategic outlook and invaluable experience in the field of Electronics and Technology Services has enabled NF Infratech to grow exponentially and emerge as a market leader in the field of ICT Services.

He spearheads strategic tie-ups and Business Development function at NFIT.

Ms. Shabana Nasim

Ms. Shabana Nasin holds over 20 years of professional experience in Technology, Infrastructure, and Consulting. She started NF Infratech in 2010 with an ambition to contribute to the Digital India transformation drive. Today, she leads an organization that employs 70+ people with presence in 9 cities across the country.
Ms. Nasim is the Head of Women Wing and Governing Body Member of the BRICS CII, an International Chamber of Commerce recognized by United Nations.
Against traditional economic and business landscape, which is largely led by men leaders, Ms. Nasim has emerged as one of the most resilient and successful women entrepreneurs in India, and an inspiration for young aspiring women leaders.

Shri Alok Sinha

Shri. Alok Sinha, IAS (retired) was in the rank of Secretary to GOI and had retired as Chairman and Managing Director of Food Corporation of India.

Mr. Sinha's strong Project Management skills, his vast array of experience and exposure as a civil servant, and his strong networks, both within the Government and the Industry have contributed significantly to the firm's growth story and its long-term sustainability.
