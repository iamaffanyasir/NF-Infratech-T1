IMPACT STORIES

Empowering Poshan Abhiyan

Distributed 600k+ smartphones, aiding women and child development across India. Enabled digitalized nutrition tracking apps, particularly benefiting Aganwadi workers in 9 states, enhancing the efficiency of Poshan Abhiyan initiatives.

Healthcare Accessibility Boost

Empowered National Health Mission with 63k+ Tablets and 226k+ Smartphones. Facilitated healthcare check-ups in underserved regions like West Bengal, Bihar, and Uttar Pradesh, strengthening the reach and effectiveness of ASHA workers in improving community...

Smartphones for Postmen

Transformed traditional postal services with 20,014 Smartphones for postmen and offices. Enhancing efficiency and connectivity, fostering digitization in India's vast postal network, enabling quicker and more streamlined service for citizens.

Banking for All

Deployed 21,100 smartphones in Phase I and 97,500 in Phase II, facilitating banking access. Empowered communities with convenient banking services, especially in remote areas, driving financial inclusion and economic empowerment.

Digital Solutions for Development

Enabled UNICEF's initiatives with 15,171 tablets in Phase I and 9,000 in Phase II. Facilitated smoother operations and data collection, aiding UNICEF's mission to improve child welfare and development globally.

Digital Tools for Societal Growth
